# Animal Prosthetics

Adds prosthetics to all animals.

Subscribe on the [Steam Workshop](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3598458723).

Source available on [GitHub](https://github.com/chronoDave/Animal-Prosthetics).

**Supported prosthetics:**
- Archotech Eye
- Archotech Arm
- Archotech Leg
- Bionic Eye
- Bionic Arm
- Bionic Leg
- Bionic Ear
- Bionic Tongue
- Bionic Jaw
- Bionic Spine
- Bionic Heart
- Bionic Stomach
- Peg Leg
- Wooden Hand
- Wooden Foot
- Simple Prosthetic Leg
- Simple Prosthetic Arm
- Simple Prosthetic Heart
- Cochlear Implant

Not all prostethics are available to all animals, e.g. a Yorkshire Terrier cannot have a Wooden Foot installed despite having "feet".
